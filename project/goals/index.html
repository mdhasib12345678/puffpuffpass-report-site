Project · Goals

What we're building this for.

Clear business and product goals that guide every technical decision in this project.

Primary business goals

  • Replace the legacy Shopify storefront with a modern, faster, fully customised ecommerce platform under our own control.
  • Migrate the existing 25,000+ SKU catalogue with zero data loss and zero SEO penalty (full URL redirect map).
  • Provide an admin experience tailored to how a Spanish growshop actually operates — multi-warehouse inventory, IVA tax handling, Spanish payment methods (Bizum), discreet shipping options.
  • Build the foundation for international expansion — ready for English customers (UK / Northern Europe) without re-platforming.

Customer-facing experience goals

  • Lightning-fast product browsing — pages load in under one second from anywhere in Europe.
  • Mobile-first design — over half of Spanish ecommerce traffic is mobile; the experience must feel native, not adapted.
  • Premium aesthetic — the brand should feel high-end, not like a generic Shopify template.
  • Discreet shopping — every touchpoint reinforces the privacy and discretion the customer expects in this category.
  • Trustworthy checkout — clear pricing, instant payment confirmation, professional order emails.

Technical & operational goals

  • 100% real-database driven — zero hard-coded content. Adding a product or category in admin updates the live site automatically.
  • Always-on infrastructure — production runs 24/7 on managed cloud, no developer laptop required.
  • Top-tier SEO from day one — Google Lighthouse SEO score of 100, structured data on every page, hreflang for multi-locale.
  • Production-grade security — HTTPS everywhere, environment-isolated secrets, encrypted customer sessions.
  • Future-ready architecture — when the catalogue grows to 50k SKUs or a second warehouse opens, no re-platforming needed.
