Project · Scope

Everything that's in scope for this build.

Comprehensive list of features, integrations and quality criteria the platform must satisfy before launch.

Storefront — public website

  • Premium responsive design across mobile, tablet, laptop, and large desktop breakpoints
  • Multi-language landing (Spanish primary, English ready)
  • Dynamic category navigation auto-generated from the product database
  • Subcategory tile strips on parent category pages
  • Sortable & filterable product grids (price, name, brand, in-stock, popularity)
  • Product detail pages with image gallery, variant selectors, real-time stock indicator, breadcrumb navigation
  • Customer wishlist (saved across sessions)
  • Real shopping cart with persistent state
  • Three-step checkout (address → shipping → review → payment)
  • Customer account dashboard (order history, saved addresses, profile)
  • Custom branded 404 + error pages

Admin panel — what the business team uses

  • Catalogue management — products, categories, collections, types, tags
  • Inventory — stock per warehouse, low-stock alerts, transfers
  • Order management — listing, fulfillment, returns, refunds, claims
  • Customer management — profiles, segmentation, communication history
  • Promotions — discount codes, time-limited campaigns, gift cards
  • Settings — regions, taxes, currencies, sales channels, users
  • Pricing — price lists, bulk updates, regional pricing rules
  • Custom branding (no third-party admin identity visible)

System integrations

  • Stripe — credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • PayPal — checkout flow
  • Bizum — Spain-specific instant payment via Redsys
  • Resend — transactional email (order confirmation, shipping, password reset)
  • Cloudflare R2 — product image hosting
  • Meilisearch — fast full-text product search at scale
  • Sentry — error monitoring (production)
  • Google Analytics 4 — traffic & conversion analytics

Quality & compliance

  • Lighthouse: Performance ≥ 90, Accessibility ≥ 90, SEO = 100, Best Practices ≥ 90
  • RGPD-compliant cookie banner & privacy policy (Spain/EU requirement)
  • WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility for keyboard navigation, screen readers, colour contrast
  • Structured data (Product, BreadcrumbList, Organization JSON-LD)
  • XML sitemap auto-generated and submitted to Google Search Console
  • robots.txt + canonical URLs + hreflang declarations
  • Spanish IVA (VAT) calculation on all transactions
